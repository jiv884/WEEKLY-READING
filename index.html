POETIC COMPUTATION

Taeyoon Choi

An artist, writer and organizer, and a co-founder of School for Poetic Computation in New York City.

"There is something poetic about code itself, the way that syntax works, the way that repetitions work, and the way that instruction becomes execution through abstraction."

This article is used in a class called Poetics and Politics of Computation, which was a class at the School for Poetic Computation.
